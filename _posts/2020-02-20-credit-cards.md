1. 拿到社保号的当天，申请首卡**[Chase Freedom Student](https://creditcards.chase.com/a1/freedom/student)** - 5,000UR开户奖励，1x返现（点）
2. 在首卡上报信用局之前，申请**[Discover it](https://www.discover.com/credit-cards/cash-back/it-card.html)** - 季度特定类别5x返现
3. 半年后，申请**[Chase Freedom](https://creditcards.chase.com/cash-back-credit-cards/freedom/card)** - 20,000UR开户奖励，季度特定类别5x返现（点）
